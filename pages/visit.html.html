Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.

These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

Location

Ripateigane 3, Ørsta, Norway

More Services

Food and drink
There is a café Attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a Ripateigane of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continueto explore science even after you’ve left the museum.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair Accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
